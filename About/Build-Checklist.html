Urania — Installation Checklist

Pepper’s Ghost Stage

  • 3D-printed TV shell assembled
  • Acrylic panel cut and mounted at correct angle
  • Smartphone fits securely, face-up
  • Hologram image centered and aligned

Sculptural Assemblage

  • Dress shirt positioned
  • Monitor placed and powered
  • Cables arranged visibly (not hidden)
  • Power strips positioned as intentional elements
  • Plastic bag and plush object placed

Photographic Prints

  • Prints mounted or installed
  • Sightlines align with hologram and sculpture

Data Visualization

  • Data visuals printed or displayed
  • Captions or labels installed

Lighting & Space

  • Ambient light controlled for hologram visibility
  • Viewer circulation path clear
  • No tripping hazards from cables
